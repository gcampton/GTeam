## How SmartRent Achieved 10 Monthly SQLs From LLMs - How SmartRent Achieved 10 Monthly SQLs From LLMs

Cover text: Single Grain identified and resolved the technical issues that were preventing SmartRent’s website from being crawled by search engines and LLMs. Targeted FAQs were added to existing pages, and high-intent transactional content was published to capture demand. This led to 136 new AI citations within 30 days, positioning SmartRent as a leader in AI-driven search results.

Categories: SEO

### Our Goal

**AI Search Visibility Growth**

Single Grain executed a series of targeted actions to implement the 5-tier priority framework:

Technical Website Audit: A deep dive into Smart Rent’s website to identify all technical web issues, from broken links to mobile performance problems.
Indexation and Redirect Strategy: Development and implementation of a structured approach to fix indexation problems and optimize redirects to improve site speed and user experience.
Core Web Vitals Optimization: A focused effort to improve the website’s performance to meet Google’s Core Web Vitals standards, enhancing user engagement and search rankings.
Content Strategy and Optimization: The AEO team at SingleGrain created and published content briefs in June and July for content intended to rank on organic and AI keywords.
Content Structure Enhancement: A thorough review and restructuring of the website’s content to incorporate FAQ’s to improve its visibility and authority in search engine results.
Keyword Mapping and On-Page AEO: Provided a new keyword mapping document, including rewritten page titles and meta descriptions, as well as strategic content insertions that use natural language processing to help them show up in LLMs.
Backlinking &amp; Web Citation Strategy: Identified key opportunities on “top 10 x solution” listicles and did strategic outreach to improve the website&#8217;s authority through obtaining placements on these articles.

### Challenges

Smart Rent’s primary challenge was to overcome the technical SEO deficiencies that were limiting pipeline growth. The core issues that needed to be addressed were:

Systematic Indexation Issues: A significant portion of Smart Rent’s valuable content was not being properly crawled and indexed by search engines, making it invisible to their target audience.
Redirect Optimization: A large number of unnecessary redirects were slowing down the website and creating a poor user experience, which in turn was hurting their search rankings.
Core Web Vitals Compliance: The website was failing Google’s Core Web Vitals tests, indicating a subpar user experience and negatively impacting their search performance.
Content Structure Enhancement: The website’s content was not well-structured, making it difficult for search engines to understand and rank their pages for relevant keywords.

### Results

Just 1 month after implementing Single Grain’s strategic priority framework, Smart Rent saw a significant increase in AI search visibility, with 136 new citations across all major AI platforms. They also achieved 10 SQLs per month, worth approximately $126,000, after implementing these changes.
