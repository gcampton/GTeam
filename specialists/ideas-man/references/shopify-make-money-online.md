# 23 Proven Ways To Make Money Online in 2026

Source: https://www.shopify.com/blog/make-money-online

## Introduction

In 2025, over 85% of US consumers shopped online, creating unprecedented opportunities for digital entrepreneurs. Whether through side hustles, passive income ventures, or full business models, individuals can monetize their skills and interests online.

---

## The 23 Methods

### 1. Sell Print-on-Demand Products

Create customized designs on white-label products without holding inventory. A third-party company handles printing, packaging, and shipping. Common items include t-shirts, mugs, and tote bags.

- **Best for:** Artists, influencers, and merchandise sellers with low investment appetite
- **Timeline:** 30-60 days to first sales with consistent marketing

### 2. Monetize a Blog

Generate income through ad revenue, affiliate marketing, sponsored posts, or product sales. Success requires regular publishing and SEO optimization.

- **Best for:** Writers and subject matter experts with niche knowledge
- **Key strategy:** Leverage social media to drive traffic while building organic search visibility

### 3. Sell Digital Products

Create ebooks, templates, music samples, and online courses deliverable via download or portal access. Digital products scale infinitely with minimal production costs.

- **Best for:** Creators and educators monetizing their expertise
- **Startup cost:** As low as $5/month for Shopify Starter plan

### 4. Become a Reseller

Source products from wholesalers, manufacturers, or secondhand sellers to resell through marketplaces or your own storefront.

- **Best for:** Entrepreneurs avoiding product development
- **Challenge:** Requires initial inventory investment and supplier relationship building

### 5. Try Affiliate Marketing

Earn commissions by recommending products through unique referral links. No product creation or shipping required.

- **Best for:** Content creators with engaged audiences
- **Key insight:** A smaller, highly engaged audience often outperforms larger but less connected ones

### 6. Become an Influencer

Build authentic communities across platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) to partner with brands for endorsements and co-branded products.

- **Best for:** Creative individuals consistently producing engaging content
- **Timeline:** 1,000 engaged followers within months with clear niche focus and quality content

### 7. Offer Transcription Services

Convert spoken audio into written documents for journalists, doctors, and lawyers. Requires quick typing skills and excellent listening abilities.

- **Best for:** People with strong typing speed seeking flexible work
- **Timeline:** First payment within two weeks of approval

### 8. Sell Photos Online

Monetize photography through specialty printing partnerships, limited-edition prints, or stock photography platforms like Unsplash.

- **Best for:** Photographers and photography enthusiasts
- **Timeline:** Approval typically takes days; immediate monetization possible

### 9. Create and Sell Handmade or Custom Products

Manufacture crafted goods with complete quality control and customization options.

- **Best for:** Makers, crafters, and artisan-focused entrepreneurs
- **Strategy:** Start with in-person sales at local markets to build awareness

### 10. Write and Sell Books or Ebooks

Publish fiction, memoirs, or instructional guides through traditional publishers or print-on-demand services.

- **Best for:** Writers and subject matter experts
- **Acceleration tip:** Repurpose existing blog content into book form

### 11. Start a Dropshipping Business

Sell products that ship directly from suppliers to customers, eliminating inventory management.

- **Best for:** Beginners with minimal upfront investment
- **Requirements:** Finding reliable suppliers, building an engaging store, and developing marketing strategies

### 12. Sell Services or Consultancy

Monetize expertise through virtual personal shopping, home décor consulting, or one-on-one coaching.

- **Best for:** Industry experts with specialized knowledge
- **Implementation:** Add booking apps to streamline client scheduling

### 13. Create Online Courses and Workshops

Package expertise into scalable digital products sold repeatedly without additional production costs.

- **Best for:** Educators, achievers, and skilled professionals
- **Development timeline:** Several weeks for comprehensive course creation

### 14. Monetize a YouTube Channel

Build audiences through consistent video content and access YouTube's Partner Program at 1,000 subscribers.

- **Best for:** Video creators and community builders
- **Timeline:** Six months to 1,000 subscribers with niche focus and consistent posting

### 15. Livestream on Twitch (or Other Platforms)

Stream gaming, creative work, or outdoor adventures. YouTube and Instagram offer comparable livestreaming capabilities.

- **Best for:** Gamers, creative creators, and interactive performers
- **Key requirement:** Consistent broadcasting schedule, often daily for several hours

### 16. Become a Social Media Consultant

Help brands develop content strategies, create engaging posts, and grow online audiences.

- **Best for:** Social media enthusiasts with proven audience-building success
- **First clients:** Source from existing professional networks through word-of-mouth

### 17. Start a Podcast

Monetize audio content through sponsorships and advertising networks. Over 584 million people worldwide listen to podcasts as of 2025.

- **Best for:** Storytellers, interviewers, and discussion facilitators
- **Success factor:** Consistent publishing schedule (e.g., weekly episodes)

### 18. Sell Products Through Online Marketplaces

Leverage platforms like Etsy, Amazon, and eBay for ready-made customer traffic without website building.

- **Best for:** Entrepreneurs seeking quick entry without marketing overhead
- **Strategy:** Use marketplace connectors to sync inventory across multiple channels

### 19. Develop and Sell Unique Products

Design original products solving common problems, then partner with manufacturers for production.

- **Best for:** Product designers and problem-solvers
- **Timeline:** Several months from initial design through market launch

### 20. Become an Online Tutor

Share knowledge in subjects like biology, mathematics, or languages through tutoring platforms or independent websites.

- **Best for:** Educators and subject matter experts
- **First clients:** Promote to family and friends' networks through Facebook and community groups

### 21. Become a Vacation Rental Host

Monetize spare bedrooms or properties through platforms like Airbnb.

- **Best for:** Property owners with available space
- **Income enhancement:** Place QR codes linking to affiliate product recommendations for guests

### 22. Narrate Audiobooks

Convert written content into professional audio productions through platforms like ACX and Bunny Studio.

- **Best for:** People with strong vocal skills and storytelling ability
- **Requirements:** Quality microphones, sound treatment, and audio editing skills

### 23. Launch a Subscription Box

Deliver curated product collections to subscribers on recurring schedules.

- **Best for:** Trend-aware entrepreneurs with niche curation skills
- **Timeline:** 90 days to generate income with dedicated product sourcing and logistics

## Avoiding Scams

Legitimate opportunities involve clear business models and genuine value exchange. Red flags include:
- Promises of large profits for minimal work
- Courses primarily profiting from participant enthusiasm rather than skill delivery

Research creator credentials and seek genuine user reviews before investing.
