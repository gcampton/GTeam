# 25 Ways to Make Money Online in 2026

Source: https://www.hostinger.com/au/tutorials/make-money-online

## Introduction

Diverse online income strategies suitable for different skill sets and interests. Key approaches include selling products through ecommerce, creating monetized content, offering freelance services, and building digital businesses.

---

## Top 5 Most Popular Methods

1. **Start an online store** — Sell physical products globally without managing inventory
2. **Create and monetize content** — Build audiences through blogs or YouTube for ad revenue and sponsorships
3. **Become an affiliate marketer** — Earn commissions promoting other companies' products
4. **Sell digital products** — Create ebooks, courses, or software for repeated sales
5. **Become a freelancer** — Offer professional skills on a project basis

---

## Detailed Methods (1–25)

### 1. Online Store
- **Earning potential:** $1,000–$10,000 monthly initially; up to $20,000 after 3+ years
- **Setup:** Use ecommerce builders like Hostinger
- **Investment:** $1,500–$13,000+ for inventory and setup

### 2. Dropshipping Business
- **Earning potential:** $1,000–$7,000+ monthly profit
- **Investment:** $1,500–$13,000
- **Process:** Partner with suppliers; they ship directly to customers

### 3. Blog Monetization
- **Earning potential:** 500,000 views can bring in $1,000–$5,000
- **Methods:** Google AdSense, sponsored content, affiliate partnerships
- **Investment:** $50–$100 for hosting and domain

### 4. Affiliate Marketing
- **Earning potential:** Average affiliate marketer earns $8,038/month; experienced marketers earn 9x more than beginners
- **Strategy:** Promote products via unique referral links
- **Investment:** Minimal (website costs)

### 5. Print-on-Demand
- **Earning potential:** Creators typically earn around $1/active listing/month
- **Investment:** $400–$500
- **Platforms:** RedBubble, custom storefronts

### 6. Streaming
- **Earning potential:** $50–$1,500 monthly; top streamers earn six figures
- **Platforms:** Twitch, YouTube
- **Equipment cost:** $500–$1,500

### 7. Amazon Selling
- **Earning potential:** Most earn $1,000+/month; top sellers exceed $100,000 monthly
- **Models:** Private label or Amazon Handmade
- **Fees:** Individual plan ($0.99/item) or Professional ($39.99/month)

### 8. Podcast Monetization
- **Earning potential:** $25–$50 for every 1,000 downloads/episode
- **Investment:** $100–$500 for equipment
- **Requirements:** Minimum 1,000 listeners for sponsorships

### 9. Influencer Marketing
- **Earning potential:** $195 to over $5,000 per sponsored post depending on follower count
- **Strategy:** Build following, partner with brands, create authentic content

### 10. Sell Photos Online
- **Earning potential:** $50–$10,000+ per sale depending on strategy
- **Platforms:** Pond5, Adobe Stock, Lightstock
- **Investment:** Camera and editing software ($100+)

### 11. Instagram Monetization
- **Earning potential:** Micro-influencers earn $300–$1,200 per brand deal
- **Methods:** Brand deals, live badges, LTK Creator (10–30% commission)
- **Requirement:** Minimum 1,000 followers

### 12. Digital Products
- **Earning potential:** $1,000 monthly or up to $30,000 in a few months
- **Products:** Ebooks, templates, courses, printables
- **Investment:** $10–$50/month for tools; website from ~$4.59/month

### 13. Online Tutoring
- **Earning potential:** Up to $38/hour depending on expertise
- **Platforms:** Wyzant, TutorMe, Preply
- **Investment:** $50–$100 for webcam

### 14. Domain Flipping
- **Earning potential:** Up to $100,000 annually full-time; hundreds monthly part-time
- **Investment:** $10–$20/year per domain
- **Timeline:** Long-term (6+ months for profits)

### 15. Sell Artwork
- **Earning potential:** $50–$10,000 per sale
- **Platforms:** Redbubble, custom storefronts
- **Investment:** $50–$150 materials and platform fees

### 16. Craft Business
- **Earning potential:** $9,700 first year to $32,000 annually
- **Platforms:** Etsy, Amazon Handmade, personal website
- **Investment:** Up to $3,000

### 17. Membership Website
- **Earning potential:** $200–$500 monthly for new sites; $1,000–$5,000 established
- **Investment:** $100–$500 (WordPress self-hosted) or $200–$400/month (cloud platforms)

### 18. Freelance Work
- **Earning potential:** Average of over $99,000/year in US; range $31,000–$275,000
- **Platforms:** Upwork, Fiverr, Toptal
- **Investment:** Minimal (website from ~$4.59/month)

### 19. Online Surveys
- **Earning potential:** Over $50/month
- **Platforms:** Swagbucks, Ipsos iSay
- **Investment:** None required

### 20. Voice-Over Services
- **Earning potential:** Commercials $100–$10,000; audiobooks $200–$300/hour; animation $100–$10,000
- **Platforms:** Voices.com, Voquent, Fiverr
- **Investment:** $100–$300 for equipment

### 21. YouTube Advertising
- **Earning potential:** ~$0.018/ad view (~$18 per 1,000 views); $1,200–$6,000/million views
- **Requirements:** 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours
- **Equipment:** $250–$600

### 22. Sell Used Items
- **Earning potential:** $500/month part-time; $10,000+/month dedicated
- **Platforms:** eBay, Depop, Poshmark, Facebook Marketplace
- **Investment:** Minimal (cleaning supplies, platform fees)

### 23. Virtual Assistant
- **Earning potential:** $10–$20 entry-level; $50–$100+ experienced (~$26/hour average)
- **Investment:** Minimal (computer, internet)

### 24. Website/App Testing
- **Earning potential:** $3–$30 per test; ~$11/hour
- **Platforms:** UserTesting, TryMyUI, Userlytics
- **Investment:** None required

### 25. Sell Unused Gift Cards
- **Earning potential:** 80–90% of card value; 50–70% for less popular cards
- **Platforms:** CardCash, GCX
- **Investment:** None

---

## Red Flags to Avoid

- Unsolicited job offers promising high pay for minimal work
- Pyramid schemes emphasizing recruitment over products
- Survey scams requesting upfront fees or personal information
- Unverified suppliers with poor reviews
- Investment schemes guaranteeing unrealistic returns

---

## Key Takeaways

- Start with methods matching your existing skills and interests
- Virtual assistance and freelancing require minimal investment and offer quick earnings
- Creative pursuits like photography or crafts leverage existing hobbies
- Success typically requires combining consistency with strategic effort
