# Monetisation Models Reference

## YouTube CPM Benchmarks by Niche

CPM = what advertisers pay per 1,000 ad impressions. RPM (revenue per mille) = what the creator actually receives (~45% of CPM after YouTube's cut).

| Niche | CPM Range (£) | RPM Range (£) | Notes |
|-------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Personal finance / investing | £12–35 | £5–16 | Highest CPM niche consistently |
| Software / SaaS reviews | £10–30 | £4–13 | B2B advertisers pay premium |
| Legal / insurance | £10–25 | £4–11 | High advertiser competition |
| Business / entrepreneurship | £8–20 | £3–9 | Strong sponsorship potential too |
| AI tools / tech tutorials | £8–18 | £3–8 | Fast-growing, CPMs still rising |
| Health & wellness | £5–12 | £2–5 | Varies widely by sub-niche |
| Fitness / workout | £4–10 | £2–4 | Supplement brands advertise heavily |
| Education / how-to | £4–8 | £2–3 | Volume game; needs high views |
| Food / cooking | £3–7 | £1.50–3 | Brand deals often better than AdSense |
| Gaming | £2–6 | £0.90–2.70 | Exceptions: finance games, high-end PC |
| Entertainment / vlogging | £1–4 | £0.45–1.80 | Need massive scale for meaningful income |

**Rule of thumb:** At 100,000 views/month, expect:
- High-CPM niche (£10 RPM): £1,000/month AdSense
- Mid-CPM niche (£4 RPM): £400/month AdSense
- Low-CPM niche (£1.50 RPM): £150/month AdSense

Monetisation doesn't rely on AdSense alone. Add sponsorships (typically £20–50 CPM for mid-size channels), affiliate links, and digital products.

## Affiliate Commission Benchmarks

### By Category

| Category | Typical Commission | Cookie | Notes |
|---------|------------------|--------|-------|
| SaaS / software | 20–40% recurring | 30–90 days | Best model: recurring = compounding income |
| Web hosting | £50–200/sale | 60–90 days | Highly competitive; hard to rank cold |
| Finance (credit cards, loans) | £50–150/lead or sale | 30 days | High regulation; verify programme rules |
| VPN / security | 30–50% or £30–80/sale | 45–90 days | Very saturated at top; sub-niches open |
| Education / courses | 30–50% | 30–60 days | High ticket = high commission |
| Health supplements | 15–30% | 30 days | Watch out for monthly recurring auto-ship models |
| Amazon Associates | 1–10% by category | 24 hours | Low commission but ubiquitous; good for physical |
| Physical products (direct) | 8–20% | 30 days | Better than Amazon; fewer restrictions |
| Travel (hotels, flights) | 2–8% | 7–30 days | High AOV compensates for low rate |

## Digital Product / Subscription Pricing

| Model | Price range | Platform | Typical conversion |
|-------|------------|---------|-------------------|
| Newsletter (paid tier) | £5–15/month | Beehiiv, Substack | 2–5% of free list |
| Micro-SaaS tool | £9–49/month | Stripe direct | Depends on utility |
| AI wrapper tool | £7–29/month | Pickaxe, Pico, custom | Varies widely |
| Info product / course | £47–997 one-time | Gumroad, Teachable | 1–3% of traffic |
| Template / asset pack | £9–49 one-time | Gumroad, Etsy | Depends on niche |
| Community / membership | £15–50/month | Circle, Skool | 2–8% of audience |

**Newsletter sponsorship rates (once established):**
- < 1,000 subscribers: too small to charge
- 1,000–5,000: £20–50 per send
- 5,000–20,000: £50–200 per send
- 20,000+: £200–1,000+ per send (niche-dependent)

## Dropshipping Margin Guide

| Cost component | Target | Warning |
|---------------|--------|---------|
| Product cost (inc. packaging) | < 25% of selling price | > 40% leaves no room for ads |
| Shipping cost | < 15% of selling price | Kills margin on low-ticket items |
| Platform fees | Amazon: ~15%, Shopify: ~2%, Etsy: ~6.5% | |
| Payment processing | ~2.9% + £0.30 | |
| Customer acquisition (paid) | ≤ 30% of selling price | |
| **Target net margin** | **≥ 20–25%** | < 15% = fragile, one ad CPM spike kills it |

**Price point sweet spot:** £25–80
- Below £25: hard to make margins work with paid ads; suits organic TikTok only
- Above £80: higher margin but higher return rate risk; needs stronger creative/trust

**Organic-only margin (TikTok / Instagram):**
- If CPA = £0, margins open up significantly
- Can work on lower-priced products (£15–40)
- Requires viral-able product with visual wow factor

## Programmatic SEO / AI Site Revenue Models

| Model | Revenue mechanism | Scale requirement |
|-------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Affiliate comparison pages | Commission per referred sale | 10k+ organic visits/month |
| Display ads (Mediavine/AdThrive) | RPM £8–30 for quality niches | 50k+ sessions/month (Mediavine minimum) |
| Google AdSense | RPM £2–8 | Works at any scale; lower RPM |
| Lead gen (sell leads to businesses) | £5–50 per lead | 500+ leads/month for buyers to take seriously |
| Directory premium listings | £20–100/month per listing | 100+ directory entries |
| Newsletter from SEO traffic | Sponsorships or paid tier | 1,000+ email subscribers |
