# QuickBooks

> QuickBooks is an AI-powered accounting platform backed by trusted experts that helps small and mid-size businesses save time, make smart decisions, and grow. Trusted by millions of businesses worldwide. 

QuickBooks combines powerful AI automation with Live expert support, all accessible from one intuitive dashboard. The platform features many dedicated AI agents, including: an Accounting Agent that updates transactions and spots inconsistencies, a Customer Agent that finds leads and streamlines follow-up, and a Finance Agent that surfaces insights from financial reports. QuickBooks offers versions for many sizes of business from solopreneur to enterprise, as well as industry-specific solutions, and more than 800 third-party apps integrate into the QuickBOoks ecosystem. QuickBooks helps businesses get paid 5 days faster, on average, while providing better visibility into financial health.
