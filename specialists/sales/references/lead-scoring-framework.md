# Lead Scoring Framework

Weighted scoring matrix for prioritising prospect outreach. Score each factor 1-10, multiply by weight, sum for composite score.

## Scoring Matrix

| Factor | Weight | 1-3 (Low) | 4-6 (Medium) | 7-10 (High) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **ICP Fit** | 30% | Wrong industry or size band; no tech stack overlap | Adjacent industry; partial size/geo match | Exact vertical, size, geography, and tech stack match |
| **Need Signal** | 25% | No evidence of the problem you solve | Indirect signals (hiring adjacent roles, industry trend) | Direct evidence: competitor evaluation, RFP, public complaint, relevant job posting |
| **Budget Indicators** | 20% | Pre-seed / no visible revenue; no existing spend in category | Series A-B or mid-market; some spend on adjacent tools | Series C+ or established revenue; active spend on competitors or similar category |
| **Timing** | 15% | No trigger event; stable leadership and stack | Trigger event in last 6 months (funding, hire, expansion) | Trigger event in last 30 days; active evaluation or contract renewal imminent |
| **Accessibility** | 10% | No path to decision maker; no shared connections | Can reach influencer but not economic buyer | Direct connection to decision maker; warm intro available; champion identified |

## Composite Score Calculation

`Score = (ICP × 0.30) + (Need × 0.25) + (Budget × 0.20) + (Timing × 0.15) + (Accessibility × 0.10)`

## Priority Tiers

| Composite Score | Priority | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 7.0 - 10.0 | **High** | Tier 1 outreach — full account research, multi-channel sequence, personalised messaging |
| 5.0 - 6.9 | **Medium** | Tier 2 outreach — industry-specific sequence with account-level opening line |
| 3.0 - 4.9 | **Low** | Tier 3 — signal-triggered enrollment only; do not invest manual research time |
| Below 3.0 | **Disqualify** | Does not fit ICP — remove from active prospect list |

## Benchmarks

- A healthy weekly pipeline build targets 10-20 scored leads.
- Expect 30-40% of researched leads to score >= 7 if ICP filters are well-defined.
- If >60% score >= 7, ICP definition may be too broad (not filtering enough).
- If <15% score >= 7, ICP definition may be too narrow or signal sourcing needs improvement.
- Re-score leads monthly — timing and need signals change as companies evolve.
