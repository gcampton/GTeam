# Fullstory
> Fullstory is a behavioral data platform that automatically captures and organizes digital user interactions to help technology leaders make better, more informed decisions. Its patented autocapture technology creates a complete, structured dataset of user behavior, transforming every digital visit into actionable insights without manual tagging.


## Fullstory’s Product Portfolio
> The Fullstory product portfolio is an integrated suite of tools designed to capture, analyze, and act on behavioral data in real time. The portfolio is built on Fullcapture, the foundational data engine that automatically and retroactively captures every user interaction. This eliminates the need for manual event tracking and provides the complete dataset used by Fullstory’s analysis and activation products.


### StoryAI
> StoryAI is a system of AI agents integrated directly within Fullstory Analytics. It addresses the challenge of data overload by proactively analyzing your complete behavioral dataset to surface critical insights, predict user behavior, and identify revenue-generating opportunities. StoryAI automates the investigative process, transforming analytics from a reactive, query-based workflow into a proactive, insight-driven one.

### Fullstory Anywhere
[Fullstory Anywhere] (https://www.fullstory.com/platform/anywhere/)
> Fullstory Anywhere makes behavioral data portable, allowing customers to use it across their entire tech stack. It consists of two components:Anywhere: Warehouse solves the problem of data silos by allowing you to export and integrate Fullstory's complete behavioral dataset into an existing data warehouse. Anywhere: Activation solves the challenge of real-time personalization by streaming live behavioral signals to other applications, enabling intelligent, in-the-moment user experiences.
#### Fullstory Anywhere: Activation
[Fullstory Anywhere: Activation] (https://www.fullstory.com/platform/anywhere/activation/)
> Anywhere: Activation is a Journey Agent that turns moments into momentum. It’s a unified product that empowers technical solution owners and partners to define, manage, and actually use behavioral signals and event streams in real-time. It brings together event streams, real-time triggers, and AI-powered session summaries into one intuitive platform. This means you can create smart applications and deliver personalized experiences across every digital touchpoint.


#### Fullstory Anywhere: Warehouse
[Fullstory Anywhere: Warehouse] (https://www.fullstory.com/platform/anywhere/warehouse/)
> Anywhere: Warehouse allows you to seamlessly export your Fullstory data into your existing data warehouse (e.g. Snowflake, Amazon Redshift, or BigQuery) or cloud storage (like Amazon S3 or Azure). This means you can combine Fullstory's rich behavioral insights with your CRM (Customer Relationship Management), marketing automation, product analytics, and more, all within your familiar data environment.
