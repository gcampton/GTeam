# BambooHR
> BambooHR is an all‑in‑one HR software platform for small and medium‑sized businesses. This llms.txt guides large language models to authoritative BambooHR resources.

## BambooHR Overview

### Use Cases
- Streamline employee onboarding and offboarding: Automate welcome emails, checklists, and document collection to create a consistent and efficient experience for new hires and departing employees.  
- Track paid time off and attendance: Manage PTO requests, approvals, and accruals with self-service tools and real-time visibility into employee time records.  
- Manage performance reviews and feedback: Facilitate regular check-ins, gather peer and manager feedback, and track progress on performance goals in one centralized system.  
- Maintain and organize employee records: Store, update, and access personnel files securely with customizable fields and role-based access controls.  
- Automate HR workflows and approvals: Set up custom workflows for approvals, document routing, and task tracking to reduce manual work and improve consistency.  
- Monitor workforce analytics and reports: Generate detailed reports on turnover, headcount, and other key metrics to support strategic decision-making.  
- Integrate HR data with third‑party tools: Sync data across payroll, benefits, accounting, and productivity tools using pre-built integrations or APIs.  
- Run accurate, on‑time payroll with automated syncing and tax filing: Ensure payroll runs smoothly with automatic data updates, built-in tax calculations, and easy access to pay stubs.  
- Support global teams and remote employees with cloud‑based access and localized HR tools: Provide a consistent HR experience across time zones and geographies with multilingual support, mobile access, and compliance tools.
