# Monthly Community Health Audit

## Activity Metrics
- [ ] DAU/MAU ratio calculated (target: >20%)
- [ ] Message volume trend (up/down/stable vs last month)
- [ ] New member join rate (up/down/stable)
- [ ] New member retention at 7 days (target: >40%)
- [ ] Channel activity distribution reviewed (any dead channels?)

## Moderation
- [ ] Moderation log reviewed (any patterns in violations?)
- [ ] Automod effectiveness checked (false positives? missed violations?)
- [ ] Moderator burnout assessed (mod activity levels, feedback collected)
- [ ] Rules still relevant? (any gaps exposed by recent incidents?)

## Engagement Quality
- [ ] Top 5 discussions this month identified (what resonated?)
- [ ] Bottom 5 channels by activity identified (archive or revive?)
- [ ] Member feedback collected (survey, suggestion channel, or DMs)
- [ ] Events held this month: [count] — attendance: [avg]

## Action Items
- [ ] Dead channels archived or merged
- [ ] One new engagement experiment planned for next month
- [ ] Moderation adjustments made (if needed)
- [ ] Health report shared with team/stakeholders
